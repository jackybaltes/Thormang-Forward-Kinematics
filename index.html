Joint 2: Left Elbow (Frontal)

Joint 3 is rotating in the frontal plane

The displacement looks like 20.0 cm to the right of the robot and 5.0 cm down.

But using Denhavit Hartenberg, we can only achieve this by a rotation along Z and a translation along X, since translation along Z will be 0.

Distance along Z axis of Joint 2 is 0.00cm, distance along X is 20.62 cm.
